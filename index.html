How to use the helper
  • Paste or type prompts into Main Tags; use Aliases and Implications to auto-correct or auto-expand tricky tags.
  • Tune Processing Options and choose a sorting mode to organize tags; drag or tap categories to mute or re-order as needed.
  • Select Sort: Illustrious Guide to format prompts as Artist/Style (optional) → Subject details → Pose & expression → Scene/background → Effects → Quality boosters, mirroring the community-proven Illustrious order (use weights like (artist:0.7) for subtle style control).
  • Review the Prompt Preview for counts, then copy or export tags in your preferred format.
